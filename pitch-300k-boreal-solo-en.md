# Boréal AI Center
## Francophone AI Infrastructure — Collège Boréal — $300,000

---

### The Vision

Collège Boréal becomes the first francophone college in Canada with production AI infrastructure, offering French-first AI tools to students, staff, and the Franco-Ontarian community.

### The Project

A complete AI center at the Sudbury campus, with GPU servers, Claw Pen platform, and training programs. All in French, data hosted locally.
