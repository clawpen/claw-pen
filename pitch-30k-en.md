# Small-Scale Francophone AI Pilot
## Collège Boréal — $30,000

---

### The Problem

Current AI tools are designed for English speakers. Northern Ontario's francophones lack access to AI tools that understand their language and culture.

### The Solution

A local AI server at Collège Boréal running French-optimized open-source models, freely accessible to students and community.
