Why We Built Claw Pen: The Case for BYOK

When we started building AI-powered tools, we kept running into the same problem: every service wanted us to upload our data to their cloud.

Code repositories, financial documents, personal notes, work files — all of it had to leave our machines and sit on someone else's servers. Even worse, we were locked into whatever AI provider the service had chosen.

The Problem with "AI as a Service"

Most AI tools today follow a simple model: you pay them, they provide the AI, and everything runs on their infrastructure. Sounds convenient, right?

But there are hidden costs:

  • Data exposure: Your files travel to third-party servers you don't control
  • Vendor lock-in: You're stuck with whatever model they support
  • Usage limits: Arbitrary caps on how much you can use "your" AI
  • Price hikes: When they raise prices, you have no alternative
  • Data retention: Who knows how long they keep your files?

A Different Approach: Infrastructure, Not Compute

Claw Pen flips this model on its head. We don't sell AI. We sell the infrastructure to run YOUR AI agents on YOUR hardware.

Here's how it works:

  • You bring your own API keys — from z.ai, OpenAI, Anthropic, or anyone else
  • You deploy pens on your own machines — your laptop, your homelab, your VPS
  • Your data stays with you — files never leave your infrastructure
  • You switch providers anytime — no lock-in, just standard containers

Why This Is Safer

When you use a hosted AI service, your data takes this journey:

Your computer → Their servers → AI provider → Their servers → Your computer

With Claw Pen, it's:

Your computer → AI provider → Your computer

The AI provider still processes your requests (that's unavoidable), but there's no middleman holding your files. No third party building a data profile on you. No service that could be breached and expose everything you've ever uploaded.

The BYOK Future

We believe the future of AI tooling isn't more hosted services — it's better infrastructure for running AI yourself. The models are getting cheaper and more capable. The API ecosystem is maturing. What's missing is easy deployment and management.

That's where Claw Pen comes in. We make it dead simple to:

  • Deploy isolated AI agents (we call them pens)
  • Manage multiple agents with different specializations
  • Keep your keys secure and your data private
  • Switch providers without rebuilding everything

Your AI agents. Your infrastructure. Your control.

That's the Claw Pen promise.
