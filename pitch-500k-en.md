# Northern Ontario Francophone AI Consortium
## Multi-Institution Regional Project — $500,000

---

### The Problem

Northern Ontario's 600,000 francophones have no AI infrastructure designed for them. Current tools are English-dominant, data leaves the region, and AI fails to understand Franco-Ontarian context.

### The Solution

A consortium of three colleges providing distributed francophone AI infrastructure across Northern Ontario. Local data, French interfaces, models adapted to regional context.
